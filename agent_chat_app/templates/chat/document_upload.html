How RAG Works
  1. Upload your documents (PDF, DOCX, XLSX, TXT)
  2. Documents are processed and split into chunks
  3. Chunks are converted to embeddings using mxbai-embed-large
  4. Embeddings are stored in ChromaDB vector database
  5. When you chat, relevant chunks are retrieved
  6. AI answers based on your document content!
